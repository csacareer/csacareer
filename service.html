What we do

Our career counselling portal offers two services for students to address their primary career guidance needs. If a student is unclear on which field of study they wish to pursue, they can make use of the portal's CheeCa system to realise a field for which they have high aptitude. Students are then provided with the field of employment that is most suited to their indicated interests as well as government scholarships and grants available for them.

About Service Learning

Service learning, is the initiative wherein the students of our institute use their skills to help society and go forward and bridge the gap between the needs of the lesser privileged and the education provided in our curriculum. We aim to provide people with tools that will get them services that at power with the state of the art technologies available around the world.
